Blog Entry 2 - Choosing a Web Development Tool

when it came time to select a Web Development tool, I researched three different tools. I began by looking at CSS Flexbox. However, I didn't think that the capabilities of Flexbox were what I was looking for. It didn't seem capable of helping me deisgn my site in the way that I imagined it looking,when I pictured it.

I realized that I imagined my site being more interactive and visually-stimulating. Because of that, I wanted to use a tool that would allow me to incorporate image changes and animations. Animate.css was a logical choice. In addition, I will probably use wow.js to make my site become active only after users take certain actions, for instance, scrolling to a certain part of the page...
