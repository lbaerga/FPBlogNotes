Blog Entry 1 - Topic & Research

I selected the topic of Autism because I have a family member with Autism and being with that person out in the world has made me aware of just how uninformed people are about Autism and its symptoms.

Sometimes, I'm on the train and I wish I had a business card or website I could refer them to, so that I could help them to learn about how difficult it can be for people with Autism to act in ways that society considers appropriate. In addition, I would let them know how hurtful it can be to be judged by people's looks and comments.

In order to research my topic, I looked at blogs, magazine articles, and scientific journals, as well as advocacy pages. I wanted to get a well-rounded understanding of Autism, so that I could see it from a scientific perspective, as well as a social, and political one.
